About Me

Professional Experience

I graduated Magna Cum Laude with a major in sociology and a minor in criminal justice. At the time, I thought of working as a data analyst, or pursuing a career in the legal field. Post undergraduate, I took programming courses including Python, HTML, CSS, and Java to refine my skills. Eventually, I became interested in IT programming and started looking for an alternative career option specializing in this field. I then discovered UI/UX design and realized that it would be a good fit for me in accordance with my sociology background. After completing a UI/ UX course, my goal is to pursue a career in UI design, where I can use my technical skills and sociology education to help meet the needs of users and create an excellent experience.
